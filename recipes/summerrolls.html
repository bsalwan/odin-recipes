SUMMER ROLLS

Description

Summer rolls are a great option for a quick and healthy lunch. See below for ingredients and recipe:

Ingredients

  • Rice paper rolls
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper (I prefer red)
  • Carrots

Directions

  1. Soak your rice paper sheet in warm water for 2 seconds and place onto flat surface
  2. Add all ingredients (should be cut into strips) to the middle of the rice paper roll
  3. Fold rice paper roll like a burrito
  4. Enjoy with peanut sauce
