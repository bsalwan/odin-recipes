ICED COFFEE

Description

Contrary to popular belief, iced coffee is a great idea all year round

Ingredients

  • Coffee beans
  • Milk of choice/li>
  • Ice
  • Flavoring syrup (I usually do caramel)
  • Optional: cinnamon

Directions

  1. Grind up your coffee beans in a coffee grinder
  2. Add water to your espresso machine and make the coffee
  3. In a glass, add your coffee, ice, milk, flavoring and stir together
  4. Optional: add cinnamon on top and enjoy!
